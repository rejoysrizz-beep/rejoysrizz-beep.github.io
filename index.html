Role-Based Permissions Help

YoyoVayo! uses a high-fidelity permission model to govern collaborative editing:

👑 Super Admin: The creator of the tree. Complete edit capabilities over everyone, plus exclusive rights to promote other members to Admins via the editing form.
🛠️ Admin: Selected family co-developers. Can edit details, add relationships, and develop the entire tree. Cannot edit the Super Admin or change roles.
👤 Regular Member: Logged-in family member. Restrained edit privileges: can only update their own profile and add/edit spouses and descendants (children, grandchildren, etc. "below them").
👁️ Guest: Unauthenticated user. View-only access.
